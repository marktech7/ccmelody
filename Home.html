EVANGELISM

This is the core of mission VISION, to share the good news of Jesus Christ with all people, especially those who have not heard or believed it. 

Discipleship

WE HELP new believers grow in their faith and obedience to Jesus Christ. 

Worship

expression of OUR love and gratitude to God for who He is and what He has done. 

Mission Vision:

Our mission is to follow the Great Commission of Jesus Christ, who called us to make disciples of all nations, baptize them in the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, and teach them to obey His commands. Our vision is to see the world transformed by the power of the gospel, and to experience the presence of God with us always, until He returns.

HISTORY AND HUMBLE BEGINNINGS:

The Church of Christ at Melody Plains was established through the dedicated efforts of the leaders of Francisco Homes Church of Christ. They shared the word of God with Jess and Venna Raralio and their family, leading to the creation of a place of worship in Melody Plains Zone 8. The church began in a simple hut, which was joyfully used for the glory of God.

When a more permanent place of worship was needed, the brethren in Francisco Homes Church of Christ raised funds to secure a property in Melody Plains. Contributions from the brethren in Melody, Francisco, and others enabled them to secure a property for the Church of Christ in Melody Plains.

Through the leadership of the church and generous donations, they were able to purchase land in Zone 2 and begin construction of the building. Despite challenges, the work continued, and the proclamation of the word of God persisted.

Over time, many families joined the church, contributing to its growth. God continued to use his servants to carry on the work, and new leaders were appointed to continue spreading God's word.

Today, the church is thriving under the leadership of Bro Romeo Bong Aspurias and other leaders appointed by God. They are dedicated to continuing the proclamation of the word of God in Melody Plains and its surrounding areas. Their mission is to reach out to those who have not yet fully accepted Him as the God who saves their lives. We pray that God will bring many souls to this place to praise, worship, and glorify His name.
